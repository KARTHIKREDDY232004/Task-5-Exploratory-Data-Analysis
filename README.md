# Exploratory Data Analysis Report

## Objective

Perform exploratory data analysis on the Sample Superstore dataset to identify trends, relationships, and business insights.

## Key Findings

1. The West region generated the highest sales, while the South region contributed the least.
2. Technology was the most profitable category, whereas Furniture showed lower profitability.
3. Most sales transactions were concentrated in lower sales ranges, with a few high-value transactions acting as outliers.
4. Profit distribution contained both high-profit and loss-making transactions.
5. Discounts showed a negative relationship with profit, indicating that excessive discounting can reduce profitability.
6. Sales and profit were positively related, but higher sales did not always guarantee higher profits.

## Conclusion

The analysis highlights opportunities to improve profitability through better discount management and increased focus on high-performing categories such as Technology. Regional performance differences also indicate potential areas for targeted business growth strategies.
